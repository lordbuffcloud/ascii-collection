# Contributing

Pull requests welcome. A few ground rules keep the collection coherent.

## Format

- Files are plain UTF-8 `.txt`.
- Pieces inside a file are separated by a line of 60 hyphens:
  ```
  ------------------------------------------------------------
  ```
- Each piece may carry an optional title line above it, prefixed with `# `.
- Preserve artist sigs. Never strip a `jgs`, `hjw`, `SSt`, or similar signature.

## Categories

Drop new pieces into the most specific existing folder. If none fit, open an issue proposing a new category before adding files so numbering stays stable.

## What not to submit

- Pieces with clear copyright claims from active artists unless you are that artist.
- Trace-overs of copyrighted logos, mascots, or branded characters.
- NSFW content.
- Anything with slurs or targeted harassment.

## What is welcome

- Originals.
- Figlet-generated banners in fonts not yet represented.
- Folklore pieces with attribution notes if you know the origin.
- Unicode box-drawing art (treated as its own subcategory under `14-borders/`).
- Terminal-ready banners for popular tools and languages.

## Style

- Width cap: 120 columns. Most renderers wrap past that.
- Monospace only. Do not submit pieces that depend on proportional spacing.
- Test that it renders correctly in at least one of: Consolas, Cascadia Mono, Menlo, DejaVu Sans Mono.
